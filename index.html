My passion

Ever since I was a little girl, I loved to draw, sketch, and create. I was fascinated by the world of fashion, and dreamed of becoming a designer someday. I admired the works of Harris Reed, who blended masculine and feminine aesthetics in his designs, and Florence Adepoju, who launched her own cosmetics brand after studying cosmetic science. I wanted to express my own style and vision, and make a positive impact on the society and the environment. I enrolled in a fashion design program at a prestigious school, where I learned from the best mentors and peers. I was ready to take on the challenges and opportunities of the fashion industry, and show the world what I could do. I was an aspiring teenage female designer, and this is my story.

Brandy Melville

Brandy Melville is an Italian clothing and fashion accessories brand, primarily targeting young women1. The brand was established in Italy by Silvio Marsan and gained most of its popularity in California1. Controversially, Brandy Melville offers clothes in only one size1. Despite this, the brand has expanded globally with stores in the US, Europe, Asia, Canada, and Australia2. It offers a range of products including tops, bottoms, and accessories, available for purchase online34. The brand’s style is often described as fashionable, youthful, and affordable.

With its finger on the pulse of youth culture, Urban Outfitters seamlessly blends fashion, music, and art into a vibrant tapestry that resonates with a diverse audience. Let’s dive into the world of Urban Outfitters and explore what makes it more than just a store—it’s a cultural phenomenon.

H&M

H&M, the global fashion powerhouse, isn’t just about clothing; it’s a canvas for creativity, a runway for self-expression, and a melting pot of trends. Let’s unravel the threads that make H&M more than a store—it’s a lifestyle, a community, and a source of inspiration.

New Look

“New Look” is a renowned British fashion brand that was established in 1969 by Tom Singh. The brand is recognized for its trendy and affordable clothing, catering to women, men, and teens. With a strong presence on the high street, “New Look” operates 489 stores in the UK and several more internationally. The brand is committed to staying ahead of fashion trends, regularly introducing new styles and looks. In addition to its physical stores, “New Look” also offers online shopping, making it easy for customers to access its latest collections and enjoy convenient deliveries and returns. The brand’s commitment to fashion-forward, accessible clothing has made it a popular choice among fashion enthusiasts worldwide.
